Timeline of Lorenzo de'Medici

1449

Lorenzo de'Medici was born in Florence in January 1449 to Piero de'Medici and Lucrezia Tornabouni.

June 1469

Lorenzo de'Medici marries Clarice Orsini, in an attempt to obtain political power with the Orsini family and their connections with the Papacy.

Decemeber 1469

Upon the death of Piero de'Medici, Lorenzo ascends to power in Florence as de-facto rulers of the republic. He intends to rule in a similar manner to his grandfather, Cosimo de'Medici, using constitutional methods. However, Francesco Guicciardini defines his regime as "that of a benevolent tyrant in a constitutional republic."

1478-1480

The Pazzi Conspiracy

1490

Lorenzo de'Medici appoints Francesco Savonarola to the convent of San Marco in order to bring prestige to the convent and its Medici patrons. Savonarola mistrusted Lorenzo and believed his patronage was contrary to religious teachings.

1492

Lorenzo de'Medici died at the longtime family villa of Careggi on the 8th April 1492.
